Study Destinations

Choose Your
Dream City in Japan

We have partnerships with top language schools and universities across Japan's most vibrant student hubs, providing you with a high-quality education and an immersive cultural experience.

🇯🇵
Tokyo
The Global Metropolis
Home to 95,000+ international students and Japan's top-ranked universities. Ideal for career-focused students.
🇯🇵
Osaka
The Kitchen of Japan
Known for friendly locals and world-famous street food. A major business hub with a relaxed atmosphere.
🇯🇵
Kyoto
The Cultural Heart
1 in 10 residents are students. Study amidst 17 UNESCO World Heritage sites in the ultimate college town.
🇯🇵
Fukuoka
The Startup Hub
A coastal "startup city" with lower cost of living and a booming tech scene. Gateway to Asia.
🇯🇵
Nagoya
The Industrial Center
Central Japan's manufacturing powerhouse. Manageable big-city experience with strong automotive ties.
🇯🇵
Sapporo
The Winter Wonderland
Famous for its annual Snow Festival and outdoor nature. Great for quieter lifestyle and language immersion.
🇯🇵
Sendai
The City of Trees
A perfect balance of urban convenience and natural beauty. Known for high educational standards.
🇯🇵
Yokohama
The Harbor City
Just 30 mins from Tokyo. Offers coastal lifestyle while remaining connected to Japan's largest job market.
